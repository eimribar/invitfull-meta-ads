### 4.3 Andromeda Concentration Problem
Both mature campaigns show the same pattern: **Andromeda concentrates 89-92% of spend on 2-3 creatives** and starves the rest. This is by design — it's optimizing for conversions. But it means:

1. Most creatives never get a fair test (< 100 impressions)
2. You can't make informed kill/keep decisions on starved ads
3. Winning creatives may not be the "best" — they're just the ones that got early traction

This is normal Andromeda behavior and is generally correct — the algorithm is usually right. But it's worth being aware of.
